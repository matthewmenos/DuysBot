// What it does

Everything a serious trader needs.

🤖
Auto & Manual Trading

Bot scans every 60 seconds and trades automatically, or switch to manual mode and tap Start Now to enter on your terms. TP/SL always closes positions automatically.

📊
7-Layer Signal Engine

RSI, EMA crossover, MACD, Bollinger Bands, volume spike, news sentiment, and CoinMarketCap data are combined into a single confidence score before any order fires.

🎯
Flexible TP / SL

Set Take Profit and Stop Loss as a percentage or a fixed price. Enable trailing stop to lock in profits as price rises. All configurable per user, per trade.

🪙
Multi-Symbol Trading

Trade up to 3 symbols simultaneously on the same exchange. Each position tracks its own TP/SL and signals independently, with no cross-contamination.

💡
Signal Suggestions

Every 5 minutes the bot scans 12 top liquid pairs on your exchange and sends a suggestion when it finds a ≥70% confidence BUY signal on a coin you're not trading yet.

🔒
AES-256 Key Encryption

API keys are encrypted at rest with Fernet AES-256 before being saved. Messages containing keys are deleted from Telegram chat immediately after entry.

📈
Daily PnL Reports

Automatic daily summaries — trades taken, win/loss count, best and worst trade, weekly running total — delivered to your Telegram at a time you choose.

🔔
Custom Price Alerts

Set price alerts on any coin with above/below conditions. Add a personal note. Get notified the moment your target is hit, then the alert clears automatically.

🚨
Panic Close

One confirmation tap closes all your open positions at market price. Admins can close all trades across all users simultaneously with the /close command.
